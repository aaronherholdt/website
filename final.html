Your Questions, Thoughtfully Answered

Essential insights about our transformative educational experience

What age groups do you serve?

Our program is designed for children ages 5-14, with classes grouped by age and/or ability to ensure an optimal learning experience.

How large are the classes?

We keep our classes small with a maximum of 4 students to ensure everyone receives personalized attention while having enough peers for meaningful collaboration.

What technology do we need?

Students need a computer or tablet with a reliable internet connection, webcam, and microphone. We use user-friendly platforms that work on most devices.

How flexible is the schedule?

We offer classes at various times to accommodate different time zones. Once enrolled, you'll select your preferred schedule from available options.

Can I cancel or reschedule classes?

You can reschedule with 24 hours' notice. You'll receive credit for 1 missed lesson per month that will apply 1 credit lesson to the following month's bill.

How do you measure progress?

We provide monthly personalized feedback reports highlighting your child's development in key skills, accomplishments, and growth areas.

Have additional inquiries? We're passionate about providing clarity!

Terms & Conditions

1. Enrollment

Your participation in Unducated is assumed to be continuous until we are notified.

2. Payment

Payment for the online class is due at the time of enrollment and on a monthly basis thereafter. You will receive a confirmation message with the confirmed dates and amount due for the next month a week before lessons commence. Payment can be made via a Thailand bank account or payment link (Payoneer).

3. Cancellations and Credits

You may cancel or pause your enrollment at any time with a 2-week notice. Example: by April 15th, you should inform us that your child will not continue for the month of May. Refunds are not provided, but you will receive lesson credit for a maximum of 2 missed lessons with at least a 24-hour notice before the lesson, each month. The lesson credit will automatically be applied to the following month's bill.

4. Class Access

The class link you receive upon enrollment will be used for every lesson of this course. It is imperative to ensure that the web browser is updated on the device used for the lesson prior to the start of the lesson.

5. Social Media Consent

Please complete the social media consent form provided. This is to obtain permission from parents or guardians to use images, videos, and/or audio recordings of their child for the purpose of promoting and sharing the online class on social media platforms.

6. Code of Conduct

You are expected to maintain a respectful and professional attitude toward instructors and other students. Any inappropriate behavior or language may result in removal from the online class without a refund.

7. Limitation of Liability

Unducated strives to provide a high-quality online learning experience for all students. However, we cannot guarantee uninterrupted or error-free access to our online platform.

8. Modification

We reserve the right to modify these terms and conditions at any time. Updated terms and conditions will be posted on our website.

Our Story

Meet the Unducated family, a group of passionate educators who value knowledge, the pursuit of learning, and the joys of travel and adventure.

At the helm is Aaron, a highly qualified Civil Engineer who found his true calling in education. With a thirst for knowledge and a love for books, Aaron constantly seeks to improve his understanding of the world and aims to inspire his students to achieve their fullest potential.

Working alongside Aaron is his wife, Vinique, a qualified brand communication specialist and teacher who manages the administrative side of Unducated. Any confirmation or administrative-related queries come straight to her. She's the backbone of the organization and ensures that everything runs smoothly.

And let's not forget about the dynamic duo, Aiden-Paul and Ava-Grace, the curious and energetic little ones who are homeschooled and encouraged to explore their interests and passions. With their inquisitive nature and endless energy, they keep the Unducated team on their toes.

The Unducated family loves to travel, seeking out new adventures and experiences to broaden their horizons and gain a deeper understanding of the world. They get excited by real-life problems and situations, which often get integrated into their courses, providing a dynamic and engaging learning experience for their students.

Together, Aaron, Vinique, Aiden-Paul, and Ava-Grace make up the Unducated family, a team dedicated to providing high-quality education and support to their students. They share their passion for learning and aim to expose students of all ages to essential 21st-century skills, all while embarking on new adventures and experiencing the world around them.
